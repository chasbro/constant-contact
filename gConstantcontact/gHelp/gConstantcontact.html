Integrate constantcontact.com contact list from word press, This widget adds a Constant Contact signup form to sidebar and allows user to add their email to site owner’s constant contact account.

== Description ==
1. Adds Constant Contact email subscribes form to your sidebar with Custom title, Custom text box caption, Custom button style, Custom text box style.
2. User entered emails are automatically stored in to site admin constant contact account (admin need to provide their own constant contact login details at widget management).
3. No page refresh, because this widget using Ajax.

== Installation ==
1. Access the site’s server via ftp or uses add new plug-in option from admin menu under plug-in tab.
2. Unpack the *.zip file and extract the /gConstantcontact/ folder and the files.
3. Upload (copy) the gConstantcontact folder to the /wp-content/plugins folder.
4. In word press administration panels, click on plug-in from the menu.
5. You should see your new gConstantcontact plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.
6. To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate.
7. Go to widget link under appearance tab and Customize/Update (Constant contat: username, password, group is must) widget to front end.

= Plugin Features =
1. Subscribe new members.
2. Add subscriber email directly into constantcontact.com contact list.
3. Identify the subscribers by using constant contact group name.

FAQ
1. Do I need a Constant Contact account for this widget?
Yes, you need to have a Constant Contact account.  If you don't have one, you can sign up for a free trial account. ["Learn more about Constant Contact"](http://www.constantcontact.com/)
2. Why sidebar display under construction text?
Because site admin (you) not enter/update the constant contact login details.
3. Where do I enter the Constant Contact login details?
Go to gConstantcontact widget management area and enter the login details.
4. Where do I update the Constant Contact Sidebar 'Top text'?
Go to gConstantcontact widget management area and update "Top text".
5. Where do I update the Constant Contact Sidebar 'Word within text box'?
Go to gConstantcontact widget management area and update "Word within text box".
6. Where do I update the Constant Contact Sidebar 'Button caption'?
Go to gConstantcontact widget management area and update "Button caption".
7. Is possible to update my textbox and button style?
Yes, you can go to gConstantcontact widget management area and update "Text Box Style" & "Button Style".
Example : "font-family:Verdana;font-size: 11px;font-weight: bold;color:#000000;background-color:#CCCCCC;"
